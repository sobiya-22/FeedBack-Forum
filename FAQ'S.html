Frequently Asked Questions

This website is a platform for Students to register their complaints related to the specific authority of the college. We aim to provide a simple and efficient way for students to voice their concerns and get them addressed.

To register a complaint, click on the ‘Register Complaint’ button on our homepage. Fill in the necessary details about your complaint. If you haven't created an account first create an account using sign-up. After filling out the required detaills related with your complaint, click ‘Submit’ to register your complaint.

After you submit a complaint, it will be reviewed by the admin and will passed to the respected authority if the complaint is valid. You will be notified the status of your complaint via Email.
